(Version 0.1)

Once file has downloaded.
Right click on it and click on the browser you would like to run it with.

You can press the Escape key to switch between Simulation and Editor.
In the editor,
- Press W or S to change what you are editing.
- Press A or D to reduce or increase the value of what you are editing.
- Read the description in red if you want to know what the selected Parameter does.
- Press E to reset the simulation.
- Press R to reset all the values back to default
